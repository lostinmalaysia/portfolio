About me

Where energy data
meets strategy

I am Syed Hamza — an analyst working in the renewable energy sector and a postgraduate student pursuing a Master's in Computer Science (Business Intelligence) at Kuala Lumpur University of Science and Technology (KLUST). My day-to-day work places me at the intersection of solar operations and data — where decisions made from insight directly affect energy output and business performance.

My interest in business intelligence stems from a simple conviction: organisations that understand their data move faster, adapt smarter, and lead longer. Working in solar has given me a front-row seat to how powerful the right dashboard or the right analysis can be — and how much value remains untapped when data is underutilised.

This portfolio is a record of that journey — from foundational BI concepts to real-world projects spanning retail analytics and renewable energy intelligence.
